ABOUT

Thank you for visiting my website. Here you can find information about myself and some of my work.

During my time in academia, I specialized in experimental particle physics and astrophysics. For my thesis, I measured light from millions of stars to deduce the color of space dust.

Lately, I have been building models of malaria parasites and their DNA to better understand how the disease spreads among people.

I like solving intricate puzzles, learning beautiful mathematical theorems, eating good food, and making music.
